Al Haramain Perfumes is based in United Arab Emirates. It producers traditional Arabian fragrances in several forms and concentrations: attars, home fragrances, incenses.

Al Haramain Perfumes is a new fragrance brand. The earliest edition was created in 2012 and the newest is from 2023. Al Haramain Perfumes fragrances were made in collaboration with perfumers Francisco Carbonnel and Christian Carbonnel.

Country: United Arab Emirates
