Ajmal, in its 60 year history of creating fragrances, has mastered the art of perfume making and occupies a unique niche in the world of perfumery in the Middle East. Today, Ajmal is a regional corporate entity with a vast portfolio of the finest and most captivating fragrances available in the region. Ajmal has established a strong presence in the Middle East with over 135 exclusive retail outlets, including 42 in the UAE, and hundreds of dealerships across the Middle East. AJMAL also exports to around 24 countries across the globe.
