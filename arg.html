Ariana Grande is an American actress and singer who started her career in a musical on Broadway, before landing the role in the TV series "Victorious" on the Nickelodeon channel. Her first album "Yours Truly" came out in 2013.

Ariana Grande is a new fragrance brand. The earliest edition was created in 2015 and the newest is from 2023. Ariana Grande fragrances were made in collaboration with perfumers Jérôme Epinette, Clement Gavarry and Frank Voelkl.
