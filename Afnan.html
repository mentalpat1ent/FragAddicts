Afnan Perfumes, established in the Ajman in 2003 by Imran Fazlani - owner and nose of the brand. Afnan Perfumes is a self-made business able to challenge the market by building unique, detailed, crafted fragrances that inspire experiences through product quality and design. This locally made, but internationally sold brand build its portfolio of products looking to share a connection to different moments and experiences in life developing the curiosity and desire of the consumer for an exotic feel of mixed ethnics.

Afnan Perfumes is brand built from passion and senses of Imran Fazlani. Being able to succeed in his goal to create a unique perfume house, Imran, with his 18 years of experience, established a one of a kind perfume house. Imran has successfully built Afnan Perfumes through an organic growth driven by life knowledge and independent motivation that inspire each collection tailor-designed and produced by conviction and direction.

 Country: United Arab Emirates
