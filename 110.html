"Escentric Molecules celebrates perfumery as the art of chemistry. The fragrances are presented in binary pairs. Each pair explores the qualities of one aroma-molecule in two different ways. The Escentric fragrance in each pair focuses on an exceptional aroma-molecule. The other ingredients in the formula are chosen to enhance this aroma-molecule, amplifying its key qualities. The Molecule fragrance in each pair takes a radically minimalist approach. It contains no odorants other than the aroma-molecule, pure and singular. Stripping away the pretension that often surrounds the fragrance world, Escentric Molecules focuses on what is truly exceptional about perfumery, the aroma-molecules that are the perfumerâ€™s palette." - a note from the brand.
